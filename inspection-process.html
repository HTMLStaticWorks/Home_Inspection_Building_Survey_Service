Methodology

The Assessment Process

A rigorous, step-by-step framework ensuring total property transparency.

1

Scheduling & Pre-Consultation

We gather preliminary data about the property (age, square footage, known issues) to deploy the right specialists and equipment. You can book instantly via our digital portal.

Book Now
2

Site Visit

Our certified master inspectors arrive on-site. We encourage buyers to attend the final 30 minutes for a direct walkthrough of major findings before the report is generated.

3

Deep-Dive Assessment

Using thermal imaging, moisture meters, and structural analysis tools, we evaluate the roof, exterior, structural integrity, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems.

4

Report Preparation

Data is synthesized into a comprehensive digital report. We categorize defects by severity and provide high-resolution visual evidence for every noted issue.

5

Client Review & Consultation

You receive the report within 24 hours. We then schedule a follow-up consultation to answer any questions, ensuring you have the intelligence needed to negotiate.
