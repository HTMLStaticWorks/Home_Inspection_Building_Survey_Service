Frequently Asked Questions

When is payment collected for the inspection?
Payment is typically collected on-site after the physical inspection is complete. We accept all major credit cards, checks, and digital payment methods for your convenience.
Can I attend the inspection with the inspector?
Absolutely. In fact, we highly encourage buyers to attend the final hour of the inspection. This allows our master inspector to walk you through the property, point out specific findings, and answer any questions you have in real-time.
How soon will I receive the final intelligence report?
Our standard turnaround time is 48 hours for Essential packages and 24 hours for Comprehensive and Premium Survey packages. You'll receive an email notification the moment your digital dashboard is populated with your report.
Do you offer a discount for multiple properties or multi-family units?
Yes, we provide custom pricing for investors purchasing multiple properties or multi-unit buildings. Please contact our commercial intelligence division for a tailored quote based on your portfolio.

Managing a Large Portfolio?

Our Commercial Intelligence division handles multi-family units, high-rises, and industrial spaces with specialized engineering teams.
