Severity Ratings System

Our reports don't just list problems; they help you prioritize them. Every defect is categorized so you know what requires immediate action and what is standard maintenance.

Safety Hazard / Major Defect

Requires immediate repair or significant negotiation. Examples: Structural failure, active roof leaks, faulty electrical panels.

Minor Defect

Should be repaired but does not immediately threaten the property. Examples: Minor plumbing drips, sticking doors, aging appliances.

Routine Maintenance

Standard upkeep required of any homeowner. Examples: Gutter cleaning needed, filter replacement, minor paint touch-ups.

Structural Blueprint Analysis

Every premium report includes an architectural breakdown. We map out exact load-bearing flaws, foundation settling, and layout anomalies directly on your property's schematic.
